## Email Best Practices

### **Subject Lines:**
- Keep under 50 characters
- Include conference name and year
- Use action words (Schedule, Visit, Don't Miss)
- Personalize when possible

### **Email Body:**
- Start with personal greeting
- Reference previous interaction
- Clear call-to-action (booking link)
- Bullet points for easy scanning
- Professional signature with contact info

### **Timing:**
- Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM (recipient's timezone)
- Avoid Mondays and Fridays
- Space emails at least 2 weeks apart

### **Personalization:**
- Use recipient's first name
- Reference previous meeting/conference
- Mention specific topics of interest
- Customize VIP emails

### **Follow-Up:**
- Track open rates and click-through rates
- Send reminders to non-responders
- Segment by engagement level
- Personalize follow-up based on actions taken
